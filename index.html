The Secret Beneath The Green

You have been invited to dinner at the beautiful estates of Lord Lucien Blackwood ルシアン・ブラックウッド卿の屋敷 , wealthy Victorian gentleman. Other guests include Dr. Watson, Lady Camilla, and Sir Henry Caldwell. Of course, his butler 執事 Miles and his Accountant 会計係 Eleanor are also in attendance. Guest of honour 主賓 , though, is none other than the world-famous detective, Sherlock Holmes.

Then, before the dinner, Lucien is found... murdered.

Now the Blackwood Estate is awash with clues, and suspicion hangs heavy in the air. As lead detectives 主任刑事たち , it is up to you to examine the evidence 証拠を調べる , interrogate the suspects 容疑者を尋問する , and help Sherlock in unmasking the killer 犯人の正体を暴く . The game is afoot!
