The Secret Beneath The Green

Lucien Blackwood, a wealthy Victorian gentleman, invites several guests to his estate, including the famous Sherlock Holmes. The other guests to his estate are Dr. Watson, Lady Camilla, his butler Miles, his accountant Eleanor, and Henry Caldwell. However, before the dinner Lucien is found... murdered. The Blackwood Estate is awash with clues, and suspicion hangs heavy in the air. As lead detectives, it is up to you to examine the evidence, interrogate the suspects, and help Sherlock in unmasking the killer. The game is afoot!
