The Secret Beneath The Green

You have been invited to dinner at the beautiful estates of Lord Lucien Blackwood, wealthy Victorian gentleman. Other guests include Dr. Watson, Lady Camilla, and Sir Henry Caldwell. Of course, his butler Miles and his accountant Eleanor are also in attendance. Guest of honour, though, is none other than the world-famous detective, Sherlock Holmes.

Then, before the dinner, Lucien is found... murdered.

Now the Blackwood Estate is awash with clues, and suspicion hangs heavy in the air. As lead detectives, it is up to you to examine the evidence, interrogate the suspects, and help Sherlock in unmasking the killer. The game is afoot!
